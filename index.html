About Us

D N S Machining Services are a family owned machining company based in Coventry. With 40+ years of industry experience from our Director, we continue to provide excellent machining services with fast turnaround. D N S have experience within the Aerospace industry as well as machine tool fitting and swift turnaround in breakdown work.

On a daily basis you will find our workshop completing work around precision components and on hand to perform any urgent breakdown work required i.e. broken shafts (repair/manufacture new ones) and bearing housings.
